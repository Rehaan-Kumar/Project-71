Campus Life

school library

Was established in 1990, when the school was built:-

Huge, Spacious Library with many desks and chairs that can fit upto 50 people. It has numerous shelves and thousands of interesting story books for kids.

School Auditorium

Was established in 1990, when the school was built:-

Huge, Spacious Auditorium with many seats that can fit upto 200 people. It has proper and elaborate lighting like a cinema hall. All of the important functions organised by our school like Concert, Speech Day, Assemblies etc. are held here.

computer lab

Was established in 1990, when the school was built:-

Huge, Spacious Lab with many desks and chairs that can fit upto 40 people. It has desktops computers for each child. There is one main computer which is connected to a projecter from which each child can see what the teacher is teaching.

science lab

Was established in 1990, when the school was built:-

Huge, Spacious Lab with equipments to conduct experiments and safety equipment for each child that is necessary to wear while conducting an experiment. It can fit in upto 50 students.

dance room

Was established in 1990, when the school was built:-

Huge, Spacious Room where students practice dance for different functions like Concert, Speech Day or other Inter-School Events. Students are also accompanied here be their teachers during dance lessons. The School is supported by Brians Academy for Dance.

school playground

Was established in 1990, when the school was built:-

Huge ground, with basketball area, football turf and cricket pitch. All Inter-School Sports Competitions organized by our school are held here. The Annual School Sports Day is also held here.

Iconic Leadership Awards 2020:-
A proud moment for HFS!!!!

The Principal of Hiranandani Foundation School, International, Mrs. Kalyani Patnaik has been recognised for her exemplary contribution in the arena of academics by the Hon’ble Governor of Maharashtra, Shri Bhagat Singh Koshyari on 12th September 2020. The programme has been presided over by the President and Chairperson of ISDI/ISMI and former Sheriff of Mumbai, Dr. Indu Shahani. It is a matter of immense prestige and unprecedented honour for the school that the Principal, Mrs. Kalyani Patnaik has been selected to become the proud recipient of the “ICONIC LEADERSHIP AWARD” this year. The award comes as a recognition for the “Exemplary Service and Dedication” in the field of education. The school is striding forward with gigantic steps and the recognition comes at an appropriate moment when the school is overcoming challenges set forth by the global pandemic in the fittest possible way. The leadership of the school is on worthy shoulders and Mrs. Kalyani Patnaik is tirelessly working to enrich and empower the faculty to grapple with online teaching

What's new?

  • New school Auditorium

    The school has built a large, spacious auditorium. It has elaborate lighting for each function. The auditorium has seats over three platforms. There are about one hundred seats in each platform thus the auditorium can fit about 300 people at once. It has comfortable seats and a large area backstage. All the functions organised by our school like Concert, assemblies, Founders Day, Speech Day, etc. and functions organised by other schools in Thane are also held in this auditorium.

  • School Introduces International Board

    The Hiranandani Foundation School, has built a New School Building a little far from the original building. While the old school continues with ICSE board, the new school offers International Board/IGCSE Board. The New School Building is doing really very well and it's name is present in the list of Top 10 schools in Mumbai and in the list of Top 20 schools in Maharashtra. From the ninth grade onwards even the children who are under the ICSE Board go to the New School Building for their 9th, 10th, 11th and 12th Grades.
